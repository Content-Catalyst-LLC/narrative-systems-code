# Hero’s Journey Film Governance Review Checklist

- Does the journey connect external movement to inner transformation?
- Is the call authentic rather than mechanical?
- Does the threshold change the story world and value system?
- Does the ordeal test values rather than merely increase spectacle?
- Does the return serve a community or responsibility beyond the hero?
- Are supporting characters more than functions in the hero's arc?
- Does the story preserve cultural specificity?
- Does the story include gender complexity and relational agency?
- Are non-heroic alternatives considered?
- Are AI-generated beats reviewed for cliché, universalism, and trope recycling?
