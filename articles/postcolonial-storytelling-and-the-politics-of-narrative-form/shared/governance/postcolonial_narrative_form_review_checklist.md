# Postcolonial Narrative Form Review Checklist

- Does the analysis treat form as political, not only theme?
- Are colonial archives treated as partial and power-laden?
- Are language politics and translation choices visible?
- Is opacity respected where appropriate?
- Are oral traditions treated as knowledge and structure?
- Is diaspora handled as braided identity rather than assimilation only?
- Are land and place treated as relation, memory, and power?
- Is gendered care and domestic memory included?
- Are AI and digital systems reviewed for colonial defaults?
- Is community governance or local authority visible where needed?
