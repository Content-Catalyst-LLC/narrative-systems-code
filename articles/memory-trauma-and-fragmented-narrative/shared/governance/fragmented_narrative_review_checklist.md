# Fragmented Narrative Review Checklist

- Is fragmentation treated as meaningful form rather than failure?
- Are silence, repetition, and gaps handled with restraint?
- Is witness consent clear?
- Is privacy protected?
- Is agency preserved?
- Is trauma extraction risk reviewed?
- Is forced coherence avoided?
- Is redemptive pressure avoided?
- Are institutional closure claims checked?
- Are digital re-use and platform risks reviewed?
- Are method limits stated?
