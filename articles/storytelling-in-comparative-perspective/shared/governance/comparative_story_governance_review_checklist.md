# Comparative Story Governance Review Checklist

- Why are these stories being compared?
- What is the unit of comparison?
- Are source contexts documented?
- Are differences preserved, not erased?
- Are translations and transmission histories reviewed?
- Are performance contexts included where relevant?
- Are archive power and collector bias acknowledged?
- Are sacred, restricted, or community-governed stories protected?
- Are local interpretations and community review included where needed?
- Are AI-generated comparisons verified by expert human review?
