# Ricoeur Narrative Time Review Checklist

- Is the temporal problem identified?
- Are Augustine and lived time handled carefully?
- Are Aristotle and plot handled carefully?
- Is threefold mimesis separated into prefiguration, configuration, and refiguration?
- Is emplotment treated as configuration, not chronology only?
- Does the analysis allow discordance, suffering, and interruption?
- Does the analysis avoid premature closure?
- Does the analysis avoid redemptive shortcut?
- Are history, memory, fiction, and testimony distinguished?
- Are method limits stated?
