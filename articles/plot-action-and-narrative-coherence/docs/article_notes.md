# Article Notes: Plot, Action, and Narrative Coherence

This article examines how plot becomes coherent through action, relation, causality, motivation, consequence, tension, transformation, and ethical responsibility.

Core themes:
- plot versus events
- action as narrative engine
- causality and consequence
- motivation and agency
- sequence and structure
- coherence without simplicity
- conflict and tension
- turning points
- ambiguity and fragmentation
- coherence failure
- ethical coherence
