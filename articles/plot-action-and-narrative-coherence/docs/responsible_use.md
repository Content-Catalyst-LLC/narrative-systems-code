# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for literary judgment, cultural expertise, legal review, trauma-informed review, historical interpretation, archival expertise, or direct community consultation.

Use them to:
- audit plot coherence
- identify detachable episodes
- review causal logic
- score action dependency
- diagnose closure pressure
- flag ethical coherence risks
- create Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about truth, blame, cultural legitimacy, trauma, legal responsibility, or historical causality.
