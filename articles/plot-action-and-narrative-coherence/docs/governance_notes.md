# Governance Notes

Review plot-coherence items when:
- plot coherence is low
- action dependency is low
- causal linkage is weak
- motivation visibility is weak
- closure pressure is high
- evidence omission is high
- uncertainty clarity is low
- audience sensitivity is high
- public consequence is high
- the story addresses trauma, public memory, institutional accountability, policy, law, history, or vulnerable groups

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- structural weakness
- evidence limits
- uncertainty concerns
- representation concerns
- revision recommendation
