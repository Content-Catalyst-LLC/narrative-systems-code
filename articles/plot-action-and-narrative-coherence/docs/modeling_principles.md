# Modeling Principles

## Plot is relation

Plot is not an event list. It is a structure of relations among actions, causes, consequences, motivations, and transformations.

## Coherence is not simplicity

A story can be complex, fragmented, nonlinear, recursive, or unresolved and still coherent.

## Action must matter

An action belongs to plot when it changes state, knowledge, pressure, relationship, or future movement.

## Risk must be governed

Coherence can clarify meaning, but it can also over-simplify causality, hide uncertainty, erase evidence, or create false closure.

## Computation supports review

Scores make structural assumptions visible. They do not replace interpretive, historical, cultural, editorial, or ethical judgment.
