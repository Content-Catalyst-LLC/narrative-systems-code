# Reversal Recognition Review Checklist

- Is the reversal prepared?
- Is the recognition supported?
- Does the reveal change meaning?
- Does the turn change action?
- Are earlier scenes reinterpreted?
- Is transformation real, partial, resisted, or false?
- Is recognition being treated as repair?
- Is evidence missing?
- Is uncertainty preserved?
- Are exposure risks reviewed?
- Is closure honest?
