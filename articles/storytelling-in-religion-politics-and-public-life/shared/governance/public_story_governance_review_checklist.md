# Public Story Governance Review Checklist

- Is the public 'we' clearly defined?
- Does the story preserve evidence and correction?
- Are opponents or outsiders dehumanized?
- Is urgency proportionate?
- Is testimony protected from extraction?
- Does civil-religious language allow critique?
- Is institutional story aligned with practice?
- Does the platform reward simplification?
- Is AI-generated rhetoric reviewed for formula, outrage, and identity hardening?
- Does the story connect hope to responsibility and repair?
