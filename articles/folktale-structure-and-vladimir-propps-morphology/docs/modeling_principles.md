# Modeling Principles

## Functions are action units

A Proppian function is an action defined by its structural role in the tale.

## Roles are not fixed characters

A character may perform several spheres of action, and one sphere of action may be performed by many different figures.

## Sequence matters

Propp’s morphology analyzes ordered movement, not merely a list of motifs.

## Variation matters

Variants may omit, compress, expand, substitute, repeat, or localize functions.

## Context matters

Morphology should be paired with performance, source, culture, language, translation, and ethical review.
