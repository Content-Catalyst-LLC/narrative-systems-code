# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for folklore expertise, community protocol, cultural review, legal review, translation review, or direct source-context research.

Use them to:
- audit function mapping
- map spheres of action
- compare variants
- evaluate sequence integrity
- identify formula-reduction risk
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to claim universal story laws, extract living traditions, erase variation, or turn folktales into generic content templates.
