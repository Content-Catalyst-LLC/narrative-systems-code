# Moral Agency Review Checklist

- Is the action named clearly?
- Is intention distinguished from consequence?
- Are harms named directly?
- Are harmed people visible?
- Is context used responsibly rather than evasively?
- Are excuse risks identified?
- Is victimhood shielding present?
- Does confession lead to repair?
- Is public moral performance separated from accountability?
- Is AI-generated moral language reviewed?
- Are method limits stated?
