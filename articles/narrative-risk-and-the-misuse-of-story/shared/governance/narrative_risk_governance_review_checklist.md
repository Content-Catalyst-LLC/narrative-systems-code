# Narrative Risk Governance Review Checklist

- Does the story preserve disciplined suspicion without closing inquiry?
- What evidence supports the claim?
- What evidence would change the conclusion?
- Are hidden-power mechanisms plausible and specific?
- Is accountability specific or shifted into broad group blame?
- Does the story use mythic simplification, enemy construction, or purity narratives?
- Are institutional failures, opacity, or historical distrust part of the context?
- Are corrections, uncertainty, and disconfirmation standards visible?
- Is synthetic evidence or AI-generated analysis involved?
- Is human review assigned?
