# Governance Notes

Review voice and perspective items when:
- perspective access is weak
- reliability risk is high
- representation gaps are high
- exposure sensitivity is high
- public consequence is high
- institutional evasion is high
- focalization shifts are unclear
- source boundaries are unstable
- point of view forces identification or false balance

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- voice/perspective issue
- access limits
- representation gaps
- exposure sensitivity
- institutional authority
- revision recommendation
