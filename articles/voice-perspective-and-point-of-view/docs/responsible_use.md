# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for literary judgment, cultural expertise, legal review, trauma-informed review, historical interpretation, or direct community consultation.

Use them to:
- audit voice consistency
- map focalization
- evaluate perspective access
- identify reliability risk
- flag representation gaps
- review institutional voice
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about truthfulness, trauma, identity, lived experience, legal responsibility, public credibility, or cultural authority.
