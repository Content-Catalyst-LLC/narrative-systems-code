# Article Notes: Voice, Perspective, and Point of View

This article examines how stories organize access to experience.

Core themes:
- narrative voice
- perspective
- point of view
- focalization
- narrator versus focalizer
- first, second, and third person
- omniscience and limited access
- reliability and unreliability
- distance, intimacy, and judgment
- multiple perspectives and polyphony
- institutional and collective voice
- media-specific point of view
- ethics of perspective
