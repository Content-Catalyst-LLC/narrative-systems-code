# Article Notes: Joseph Campbell and the Comparative Study of Myth

This article examines Joseph Campbell as a major public interpreter of comparative mythology, the monomyth, archetypal interpretation, and symbolic storytelling.

Core themes:
- Campbell's role in popularizing comparative mythology
- The Hero with a Thousand Faces
- the monomyth before formula drift
- Campbell and archetypal interpretation
- sources, influences, and intellectual context
- Campbell's influence on film and modern storytelling
- strengths of Campbell's method
- critiques involving universalism, gender, source selection, and cultural specificity
- responsible use of Campbell as one interpretive lens
