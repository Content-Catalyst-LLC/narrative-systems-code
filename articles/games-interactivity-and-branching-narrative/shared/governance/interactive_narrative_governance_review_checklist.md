# Interactive Narrative Governance Review Checklist

- Do player choices create meaningful effects?
- Does the system respond clearly and fairly?
- Does the world remember important actions?
- Do mechanics support the story's values?
- Are branches sustainable, tested, and coherent?
- Does failure support meaning rather than only friction?
- Are identity systems careful and non-stereotyped?
- Does monetization distort narrative progress or consent?
- Are AI-generated quests transparent and reviewed?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
