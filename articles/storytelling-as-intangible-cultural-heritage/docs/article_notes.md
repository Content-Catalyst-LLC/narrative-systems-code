# Article Notes: Storytelling as Intangible Cultural Heritage

This article examines storytelling as living cultural heritage rather than detachable content.

Core themes:

- intangible cultural heritage
- oral tradition and language
- performance, ritual, and community context
- memory, identity, and continuity
- safeguarding without freezing
- documentation and archive risk
- digital context collapse
- education, tourism, and public heritage
- ownership, consent, and community authority
- heritage, power, and representation
- ethics of safeguarding
