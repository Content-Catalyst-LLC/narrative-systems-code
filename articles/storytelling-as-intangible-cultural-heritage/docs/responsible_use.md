# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for community protocol, cultural review, legal review, archival ethics, translation review, trauma-informed review, or direct consent.

Use them to:
- audit living continuity
- assess safeguarding readiness
- preserve heritage context
- document consent and access controls
- identify archive and platform risks
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate decisions about sacred material, restricted knowledge, community ownership, or permission to publish.
