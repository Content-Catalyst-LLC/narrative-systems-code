# Governance Notes

Review storytelling heritage items when:
- safeguarding readiness is weak
- consent clarity is weak
- access control is weak
- restricted material is present
- performance context is missing
- translation loss is likely
- extraction risk is high
- platform exposure is likely
- community governance is weak
- public consequence is high

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- heritage context
- consent layers
- access level
- archive risk
- community authority
- benefit sharing
- revision recommendation
