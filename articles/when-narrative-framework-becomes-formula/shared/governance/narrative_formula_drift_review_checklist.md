# Narrative Formula Drift Review Checklist

- Is the framework named clearly?
- Is the framework scope visible?
- Is the model being used descriptively or prescriptively?
- Are alternative lenses tested?
- Is story refusal treated as meaningful?
- Is closure pressure too high?
- Are characters becoming functions?
- Is cultural or relational form being flattened?
- Are AI outputs checked for generic arcs?
- Is human judgment still in control?
