# Life-Writing Review Checklist

- Is the life-writing form identified?
- Is the scope clear?
- Are memory, fact, interpretation, and evidence distinguished?
- Is the autobiographical I analyzed?
- Are other people represented fairly?
- Are consent and privacy issues reviewed?
- Is trauma handled without extraction or forced redemption?
- Is self-mythology checked?
- Are digital and AI mediations reviewed?
- Are method limits stated?
