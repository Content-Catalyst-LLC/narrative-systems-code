# Institutional Memory Governance Review Checklist

- Is the founding claim clearly stated?
- Is legitimacy being asserted or evidenced?
- Are records and archives available for review?
- Are affected communities represented as witnesses or co-authors?
- Is founder heroization hiding collective labor or harm?
- Are exclusion, displacement, conflict, and dissent visible?
- Does reform language connect to measurable repair?
- Are metadata and public access adequate?
- Is AI summarization reviewed for smoothing, source omission, and archive bias?
- Does the origin story remain revisable?
