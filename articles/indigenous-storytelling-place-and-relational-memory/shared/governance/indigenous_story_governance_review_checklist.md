# Indigenous Story Governance Review Checklist

- Is the People, Nation, language, and place named only where appropriate and permitted?
- Does the analysis avoid pan-Indigenous generalization?
- Is teller authority visible?
- Is listener responsibility visible?
- Are seasonal, ceremonial, family, or community protocols checked?
- Is place treated as relation, memory, law, or participant rather than scenery?
- Are language, untranslatability, opacity, and translation limits visible?
- Are archive access and digitization treated as governance questions?
- Are AI training risk, metadata flattening, and stereotype bias reviewed?
- Is the limit of use clearly stated?
