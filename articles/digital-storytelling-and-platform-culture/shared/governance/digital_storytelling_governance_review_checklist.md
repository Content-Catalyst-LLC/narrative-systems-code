# Digital Storytelling Governance Review Checklist

- Is context preserved when the story circulates?
- Are source authority and provenance clear?
- Is audience spread intentional and safe?
- Could compression create context collapse?
- Are metrics helping judgment or replacing it?
- Is the story over-shaped by platform trends?
- Are archive metadata, consent, and preservation addressed?
- Is synthetic media disclosed and reviewed?
- Are vulnerable people or communities protected?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
