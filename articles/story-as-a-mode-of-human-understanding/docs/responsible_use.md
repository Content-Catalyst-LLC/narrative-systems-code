# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for cultural expertise, ethical review, legal review, historical interpretation, clinical judgment, or direct community consultation.

Use them to:
- audit story as a mode of understanding
- identify causal claims and agency assumptions
- evaluate moral understanding and overreach risk
- create Canvas-ready governance queues
- support editorial review

Do not use them to automate judgments about identity, trauma, law, medicine, political responsibility, cultural authenticity, or historical truth.
