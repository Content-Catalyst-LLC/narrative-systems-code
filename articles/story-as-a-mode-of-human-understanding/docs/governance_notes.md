# Governance Notes

Review narrative-understanding items when:
- evidence support is low
- causal framing is weak
- agency mapping is incomplete
- hindsight bias is high
- false coherence is high
- selection bias is high
- closure pressure is high
- the story assigns blame, harm, identity, public meaning, or moral responsibility

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- evidence limitations
- causal assumptions
- omitted perspectives
- agency constraints
- uncertainty
- revision recommendations
