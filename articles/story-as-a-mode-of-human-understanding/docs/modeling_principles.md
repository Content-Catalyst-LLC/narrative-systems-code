# Modeling Principles

## Story structures understanding

Narrative helps connect events, motives, memory, causality, identity, and possible futures.

## Narrative understanding is interpretive

A story is not a neutral container. It selects, arranges, frames, and emphasizes.

## Coherence is not proof

A story can feel coherent while evidence remains weak or causality remains uncertain.

## Agency must be mapped carefully

Narrative analysis should distinguish actors, constraints, institutions, systems, beneficiaries, harms, and omissions.

## Computation supports interpretation

Scores help reveal assumptions and prioritize review. They do not determine meaning, truth, or moral responsibility.
