# Article Notes: Story as a Mode of Human Understanding

This article examines story as a cognitive, cultural, moral, and interpretive structure.

Core themes:
- story and human understanding
- narrative versus information
- time, sequence, and meaning
- causality and consequence
- motives, agency, and action
- memory and interpretation
- identity and self-understanding
- moral understanding
- possible worlds
- social and cultural understanding
- story in knowledge systems
- limits of narrative understanding
