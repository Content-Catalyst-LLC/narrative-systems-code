# Public Narrative Governance Review Checklist

- Does the story clearly connect self, us, now, values, and action?
- Is the requested action feasible and connected to a change pathway?
- Are affected communities represented with authority and agency?
- Is testimony used with consent, safety, context, and reuse limits?
- Does the story build coalition without erasing difference?
- Are digital circulation risks and context collapse addressed?
- Are AI-generated messages reviewed for omitted voices and false consensus?
- Is evidence visible?
- Is uncertainty preserved?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
