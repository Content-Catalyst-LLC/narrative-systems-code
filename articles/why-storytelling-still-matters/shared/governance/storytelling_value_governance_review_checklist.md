# Storytelling Value Governance Review Checklist

- Why is this story being told?
- What public, educational, cultural, or institutional value does it provide?
- Are claims, examples, images, and causes truthful?
- Are context, uncertainty, and limits visible?
- Are represented people treated with dignity, consent, and agency?
- Does the story support audience reasoning rather than replace it?
- Does the story avoid oversimplification, scapegoating, and manipulation?
- Are AI use, provenance, and source traceability disclosed?
- Is human review assigned?
- Is there a correction or revision pathway?
