# Heroine Journey Review Checklist

- Is the model sourced to Maureen Murdock?
- Are stages used as prompts rather than required beats?
- Is gender essentialism avoided?
- Are universal womanhood assumptions avoided?
- Are cultural and intersectional contexts visible?
- Is the mother or feminine not reduced to symbol only?
- Is descent not romanticized?
- Is integration not claimed too quickly?
- Are alternative narrative frameworks considered?
- Is AI stage assignment reviewed for uncertainty?
