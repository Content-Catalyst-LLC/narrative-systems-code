# Article Notes: Performance, Memory, and Variation in Oral Storytelling

This article examines oral storytelling as a living performance system rather than a static text.

Core themes:
- storytelling as performance event
- performance as composition
- memory as practice
- variation as continuity and adaptation
- formula, repetition, and recognition
- audience and occasion
- voice, gesture, and embodiment
- transmission and apprenticeship
- stability and change
- archive risk and platform circulation
- ethics of documenting variation
