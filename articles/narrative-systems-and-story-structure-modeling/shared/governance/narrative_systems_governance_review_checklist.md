# Narrative Systems Governance Review Checklist

- Is the model explaining structure or imposing formula?
- Are causal links supported by evidence?
- Are state transitions clear?
- Are agents, goals, constraints, and world rules visible?
- Does the model preserve temporal architecture and audience knowledge?
- Are individual and systemic agency both visible where appropriate?
- Are relationships, networks, and feedback loops modeled?
- Does the model avoid reducing characters to functions?
- Does AI output invent causality or default to generic beats?
- Is human review assigned?
