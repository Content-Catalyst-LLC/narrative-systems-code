# Alternative Structure Review Checklist

- Is the story being forced into a heroic quest?
- Is conflict being substituted for other forms of movement?
- Is return or closure being pressured?
- Is agency individual, relational, distributed, ecological, institutional, or interactive?
- Does the structure match the medium?
- Are gaps, fragments, cycles, care labor, and archive logic preserved?
- Are cultural forms handled with context?
- Are AI-generated outlines checked for conventional plot bias?
- Are method limits stated?
