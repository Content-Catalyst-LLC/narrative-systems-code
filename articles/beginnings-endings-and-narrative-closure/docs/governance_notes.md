# Governance Notes

Review beginning-and-closure items when:
- opening clarity is weak
- the opening promise is abandoned
- closure integrity is low
- beginning-ending alignment is weak
- unresolved consequence is omitted
- closure risk is high
- premature repair appears
- false resolution appears
- system flattening appears
- audience comfort replaces accountability

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- opening promise
- ending type
- closure form
- unresolved consequences
- ethical risk
- revision recommendation
