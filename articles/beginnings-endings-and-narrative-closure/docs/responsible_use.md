# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for literary judgment, trauma-informed review, legal review, cultural expertise, historical interpretation, institutional accountability, or direct community consultation.

Use them to:
- audit opening clarity
- track narrative promise
- evaluate closure integrity
- map beginning-ending alignment
- identify unresolved consequences
- flag closure risk
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about trauma, justice, repair, public accountability, legal responsibility, or historical closure.
