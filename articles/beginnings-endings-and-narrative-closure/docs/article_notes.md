# Article Notes: Beginnings, Endings, and Narrative Closure

This article examines how stories frame entry, establish expectation, and create completion, suspension, or continuation.

Core themes:
- beginnings as framing
- opening orientation and disorientation
- opening promise and narrative contract
- endings as interpretation
- closure versus resolution versus completion
- circular return and transformation
- open endings and unresolved consequence
- seriality and continuation
- retrospection and afterwardness
- ethics of closure
