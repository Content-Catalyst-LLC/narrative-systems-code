# Governance Notes

Review cultural story items when:
- cultural context is thin
- source transparency is low
- representation care is low
- persuasive intensity is high
- public impact is high
- the story carries identity, trauma, cultural memory, political meaning, or institutional legitimacy
- the story may exclude, appropriate, stereotype, scapegoat, or manipulate

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- status
- cultural context
- source limits
- representation cautions
- audience consequences
- revision recommendations
