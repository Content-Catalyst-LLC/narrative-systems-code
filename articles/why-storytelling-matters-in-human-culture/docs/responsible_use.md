# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for cultural expertise, ethical review, community consultation, legal review, or editorial judgment.

Use them to:
- audit cultural story functions
- identify memory and teaching roles
- score transmission strength
- detect narrative risk
- document representation cautions
- create Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about cultures, communities, identity, trauma, religion, law, public memory, or political legitimacy.
