# Article Notes: Why Storytelling Matters in Human Culture

This article examines storytelling as cultural memory, teaching, identity formation, belonging, moral imagination, social coordination, emotional life, public meaning, and ethical responsibility.

Core themes:
- story as cultural memory
- story as teaching
- moral imagination
- personal and collective identity
- belonging and exclusion
- public narrative
- oral tradition and transmission
- storytelling and power
- ethical risks of narrative
