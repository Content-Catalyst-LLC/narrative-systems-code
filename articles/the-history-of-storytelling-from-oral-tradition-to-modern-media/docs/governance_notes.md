# Governance Notes

Review historical storytelling items when:
- context retention is low
- archive durability is weak
- platform stability is weak
- access is restricted
- institutional dependence is high
- collection, translation, or extraction risk is present
- cultural ownership or permission is unclear

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- medium
- historical period
- authority structure
- preservation risk
- access barriers
- interpretive cautions
- revision recommendations
