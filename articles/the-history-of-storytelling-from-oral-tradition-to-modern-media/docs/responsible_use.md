# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for cultural expertise, archival expertise, community consultation, legal review, or ethical judgment.

Use them to:
- compare story media historically
- audit transmission strength
- identify preservation risk
- document context loss
- examine access and authority
- create Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about cultural value, authenticity, ownership, sacred traditions, community memory, or historical truth.
