# Article Notes: The History of Storytelling from Oral Tradition to Modern Media

This article examines storytelling history through media systems rather than a simple technological timeline.

Core themes:
- oral tradition and embodied performance
- myth, ritual, epic, and collective memory
- writing and manuscript preservation
- print circulation and seriality
- theater and public performance
- novels and interiority
- photography and film
- radio and television
- digital platforms and networked storytelling
- games and interactivity
- transmedia storytelling
- archives, access, and power
