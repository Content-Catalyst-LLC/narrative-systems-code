# Historical Storytelling Review Checklist

- Is the medium clearly identified?
- Is the historical period explicit?
- Is performance or publication context documented?
- Are teller, author, scribe, collector, editor, translator, publisher, or platform roles distinguished?
- Are preservation risks named?
- Are access barriers named?
- Is cultural context retained?
- Are community protocols or permissions relevant?
- Is archive or platform dependence documented?
