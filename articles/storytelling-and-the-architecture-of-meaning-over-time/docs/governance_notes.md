# Governance Notes

Review meaning-architecture items when:
- drift risk is high
- context retention is low
- source age is high
- link breakage is high
- repetition is strong but evidence is weak
- continuity claims ignore rupture
- rupture claims erase continuity
- representation risk is high
- audience consequence is high
- the story anchors a series, institution, memory system, or public-facing knowledge architecture

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- temporal layer
- source limits
- context-retention issues
- continuity and rupture notes
- revision recommendations
