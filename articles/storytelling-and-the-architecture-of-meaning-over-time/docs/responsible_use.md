# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for cultural expertise, ethical review, legal review, historical interpretation, archival expertise, or direct community consultation.

Use them to:
- audit temporal coherence
- identify memory durability and drift risk
- prioritize revision
- document continuity, rupture, and context
- create Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about cultural memory, trauma, political responsibility, institutional legitimacy, sacred history, or historical truth.
