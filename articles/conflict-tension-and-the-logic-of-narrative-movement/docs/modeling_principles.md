# Modeling Principles

## Conflict is structural pressure

Conflict is not merely loudness, violence, argument, or dramatic surface. It is a relation of pressure, opposition, constraint, incompatibility, or unresolved stakes.

## Tension is pressure over time

Tension lasts when unresolved pressure develops, deepens, shifts, narrows, escalates, or transforms.

## Movement requires change

Narrative movement occurs when conflict changes state, knowledge, pressure, relationship, future possibility, or value.

## Conflict must be governed

Conflict can clarify stakes, but it can also create scapegoating, false balance, conflict inflation, trauma spectacle, villain simplification, or premature closure.
