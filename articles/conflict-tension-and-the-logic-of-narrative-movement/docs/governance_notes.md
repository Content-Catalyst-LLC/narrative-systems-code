# Governance Notes

Review conflict-and-tension items when:
- conflict clarity is low
- tension durability is weak
- narrative movement is low
- scapegoating is high
- conflict inflation is high
- trauma spectacle is high
- false balance is high
- closure pressure is high
- audience sensitivity is high
- public consequence is high

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- conflict type
- pressure relation
- stakes
- movement produced
- ethical risks
- evidence or context limits
- revision recommendation
