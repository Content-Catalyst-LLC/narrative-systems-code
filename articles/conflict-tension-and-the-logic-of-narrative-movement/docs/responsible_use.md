# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for literary judgment, cultural expertise, trauma-informed review, legal review, political judgment, historical interpretation, or direct community consultation.

Use them to:
- audit conflict clarity
- evaluate tension durability
- score narrative movement
- identify stakes and pressure
- flag conflict-risk concerns
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about blame, identity, harm, trauma, responsibility, public controversy, or historical conflict.
