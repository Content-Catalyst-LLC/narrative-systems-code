# Article Notes: Conflict, Tension, and the Logic of Narrative Movement

This article examines how stories move through pressure.

Core themes:
- conflict versus tension
- narrative movement versus noise
- desire, obstacle, and pressure
- stakes and consequence
- internal, interpersonal, moral, institutional, systemic, and epistemic conflict
- tension over time
- escalation, reversal, release, and suspension
- ethical risks of conflict
