# Conflict and Tension Review Checklist

- Is the conflict relation clear?
- Are desire and obstacle visible?
- Are stakes concrete?
- Does tension develop rather than repeat?
- Does the conflict create movement?
- Is escalation meaningful?
- Is release earned?
- Is scapegoating avoided?
- Is conflict inflation avoided?
- Is trauma handled with care?
- Is false balance avoided?
- Is closure honest?
