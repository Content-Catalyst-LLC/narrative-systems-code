# Organizational Story Governance Review Checklist

- Does the official story match decisions, budgets, incentives, and working conditions?
- Does the change story name loss, uncertainty, resources, participation, and accountability?
- Are employee stories protected from extraction?
- Are stakeholder stories gathered with consent and context?
- Are counterstories treated as evidence?
- Are failure and dissent preserved in organizational memory?
- Do dashboards or AI summaries hide uncertainty or omitted dissent?
- Is privacy protected?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
- Does the story create measurable obligations?
