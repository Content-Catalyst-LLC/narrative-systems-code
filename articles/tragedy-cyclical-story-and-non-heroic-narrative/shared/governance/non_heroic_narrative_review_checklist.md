# Non-Heroic Narrative Review Checklist

- Is tragedy being treated as failed heroism?
- Is hamartia reduced too simply to personality flaw?
- Is catharsis replacing accountability?
- Is repetition being misread as weak plot?
- Is care labor visible as narrative action?
- Are witness and refusal preserved?
- Is systemic harm being individualized?
- Is ecological or cyclical time flattened?
- Is the ending being forced into false closure?
- Are AI-generated structures checked for heroic template forcing?
