# Rhetorical Moves Governance Review Checklist

- What does the story ask the audience to believe, feel, trust, share, buy, or do?
- Are persuasive purpose and sponsorship clear?
- Are evidence, examples, and causal claims accurate and contextualized?
- Is emotional intensity proportionate to evidence?
- Are represented people treated with dignity, consent, and agency?
- Can the audience inspect evidence and consider alternatives?
- Are urgency and social proof used responsibly?
- Does platform circulation amplify pressure or context collapse?
- Does AI personalization target vulnerability?
- Is human review assigned?
