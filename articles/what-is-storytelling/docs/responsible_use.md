# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for cultural expertise, ethical review, community consultation, legal review, or editorial judgment.

Use them to:
- audit narrative structure
- detect weak transformation logic
- identify representation risks
- document evidence quality
- build Canvas-ready story metadata
- create governance queues for human review

Do not use them to automate high-stakes judgments about people, cultures, identity, trauma, law, or public memory.
