# Governance Notes

Review narrative items when:
- evidence strength is low
- representation care is low
- persuasive intensity is high
- audience consequence is high
- omitted voices are likely
- cultural interpretation is sensitive
- the story involves harm, trauma, identity, public memory, or vulnerable groups

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- status
- evidence limits
- interpretive cautions
- representation cautions
- recommended revision
