# Universal Story Model Review Checklist

- Is the model claim proportional?
- Is the source archive visible?
- Are gender assumptions reviewed?
- Is intersectional context included?
- Are queer, trans, and nonbinary narrative pressures reviewed?
- Is local or cultural authority preserved?
- Are alternative structures considered?
- Is care, witness, relation, refusal, or cyclicality visible where relevant?
- Is AI stage assignment flagged for uncertainty?
- Are method limits stated?
