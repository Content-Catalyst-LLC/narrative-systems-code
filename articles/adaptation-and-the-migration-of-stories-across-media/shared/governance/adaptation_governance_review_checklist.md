# Adaptation Governance Review Checklist

- What is the source material?
- What is the target medium?
- What core story function is being preserved?
- What is transformed, compressed, expanded, or omitted?
- What voice, interiority, context, provenance, or agency is lost?
- Who owns or authorizes the adaptation?
- Are source communities or affected people credited and included?
- Does franchise expansion serve story purpose or brand recognition?
- Are AI-generated treatments reviewed for source flattening and synthetic opacity?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
