# Representation Ethics Review Checklist

- Who is represented?
- Who controls the narrative frame?
- Are represented people given voice and agency?
- Is consent informed, ongoing, and specific to circulation?
- Is platform visibility safe and intentional?
- Does the story avoid stereotype and victim-only portrayal?
- Are testimony and trauma handled without extraction?
- Are images necessary, dignified, contextualized, and captioned accurately?
- Are cultural protocols, attribution, and community review addressed?
- Is AI-generated representation disclosed and reviewed?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
