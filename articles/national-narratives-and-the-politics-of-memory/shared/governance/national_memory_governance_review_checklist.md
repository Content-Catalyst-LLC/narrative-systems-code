# National Memory Governance Review Checklist

- Is the national "we" clearly defined?
- Does the story preserve evidence and correction?
- Does the story make room for multiple communities' memories?
- Are monuments, holidays, and textbooks contextualized?
- Is countermemory treated as evidence rather than disloyalty?
- Does commemoration connect to repair?
- Are archives and records accessible?
- Are digital materials sourced and contextualized?
- Is AI summarization reviewed for dominant-archive bias and omitted minority memory?
- Does the narrative remain contestable?
