# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for community protocol, language expertise, performer authority, cultural review, legal review, archival ethics, or direct consent.

Use them to:
- audit oral-form context
- preserve sound and repetition notes
- assess ritual authority
- identify archive and platform risks
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to publish restricted ritual speech, quote sacred material without review, or treat online availability as ethical permission.
