# Governance Notes

Review compact oral forms when:
- ritual authority is unclear
- consent status is weak
- access control is weak
- sound documentation is missing
- translation loss is likely
- quote extraction risk is high
- platform circulation is involved
- restricted material may be present
- adaptation or sampling is planned

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- oral form
- context
- sound and repetition
- access protocol
- authority notes
- archive risk
- revision recommendation
