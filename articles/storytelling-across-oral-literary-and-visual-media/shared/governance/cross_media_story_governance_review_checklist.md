# Cross-Media Story Governance Review Checklist

- What was the original medium?
- What medium is the story moving into?
- What voice, gesture, context, or audience relation is lost?
- Is source authority documented?
- Is consent documented for adaptation, voice, likeness, images, and circulation?
- Is provenance visible?
- Are synthetic or AI-generated elements disclosed?
- Does the new medium create documentary ambiguity?
- Does the transfer distort cultural context?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
