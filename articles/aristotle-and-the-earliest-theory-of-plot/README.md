# Aristotle and the Earliest Theory of Plot

This companion directory supports the article **"Aristotle and the Earliest Theory of Plot."**

The workflows operationalize Aristotle's theory of plot as a structured-action audit. They include plot-unity scoring, causal-linkage diagnostics, beginning-middle-end checks, reversal and recognition mapping, character-action integration, formula-risk scoring, genre and medium fit checks, plot governance queues, and Catalyst Canvas-ready JSON exports.

## Core Claim

Aristotle's *Poetics* gives one of the earliest surviving theories of plot as organized action. Plot is not merely what happens; it is the arrangement of incidents into a whole action that has beginning, middle, end, unity, probability, necessity, consequence, reversal, recognition, and emotional effect.
