# Modeling Principles

## Plot is arrangement

Plot is not a loose list of incidents. It is the arrangement of incidents into meaningful action.

## Unity is structural

Unity of action asks whether incidents belong to one developing action, not whether one hero appears throughout.

## Surprise must be earned

Reversal and recognition can surprise audiences while still following through probability or necessity.

## Character acts

Character matters because people choose, act, suffer, recognize, and change within the plot.

## Formula risk must be governed

Aristotelian analysis should clarify plot structure without forcing all stories into Greek tragic criteria or modern formula templates.
