# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for literary judgment, cultural expertise, dramaturgical expertise, ethical review, historical interpretation, or direct textual analysis.

Use them to:
- audit plot structure
- identify detachable episodes
- review causal logic
- map reversal and recognition
- diagnose formula risk
- create Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to rank cultural value, enforce one universal plot model, or dismiss non-Aristotelian storytelling forms.
