# Governance Notes

Review Aristotelian plot-analysis items when:
- unity is low
- causal linkage is weak
- episodes are detachable
- reversal is unprepared
- recognition is informational but not consequential
- character is detached from action
- formula risk is high
- genre or medium fit is low
- cultural awareness is low

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- plot concept under review
- evidence or text basis
- formula risk
- medium and genre cautions
- revision recommendations
