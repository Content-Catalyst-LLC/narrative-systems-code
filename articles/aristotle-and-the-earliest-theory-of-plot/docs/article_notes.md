# Article Notes: Aristotle and the Earliest Theory of Plot

This article examines Aristotle's *Poetics* as an early theory of plot and structured action.

Core themes:
- *Poetics* as analysis of making
- mimesis and imitation of action
- plot as *muthos*
- beginning, middle, and end
- unity of action
- probability and necessity
- reversal, recognition, and suffering
- character and plot
- catharsis and emotional effect
- formula risk and later misreadings
- limits of applying Aristotle universally
