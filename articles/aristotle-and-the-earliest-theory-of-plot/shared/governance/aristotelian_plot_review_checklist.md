# Aristotelian Plot Review Checklist

- Is the central action explicit?
- Are incidents arranged rather than merely listed?
- Does the story begin where the action begins?
- Does the middle develop consequence?
- Does the ending complete or intentionally withhold completion?
- Are episodes detachable?
- Does reversal follow from prior action?
- Does recognition change consequence?
- Is character revealed through action?
- Is Aristotle being used as analysis rather than formula?
