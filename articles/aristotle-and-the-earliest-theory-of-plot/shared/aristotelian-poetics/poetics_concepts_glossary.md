# Poetics Concepts Glossary

- mimesis: representation or imitation through artistic form
- muthos: plot or arrangement of incidents
- peripeteia: reversal
- anagnorisis: recognition
- pathos: suffering or destructive action
- catharsis: debated term tied to tragic emotional effect
- unity of action: structural belonging of parts to one complete action
- probability and necessity: logic through which plot events follow
