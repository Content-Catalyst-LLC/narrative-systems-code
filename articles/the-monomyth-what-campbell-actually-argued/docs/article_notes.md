# Article Notes: The Monomyth: What Campbell Actually Argued

This article distinguishes Campbell's actual monomyth argument from later formulaic uses of the hero's journey.

Core themes:
- monomyth as symbolic model, not mechanical formula
- departure, initiation, and return
- threshold crossing
- symbolic death and transformation
- boon and return
- what Campbell did and did not argue
- formula drift
- strengths and limits
- gender, community, and alternative story structures
- responsible use, counterexamples, and governance
