# Modeling Principles

## Model the claim, not the myth

The workflow audits claims about monomythic structure. It does not determine the meaning of a myth.

## Symbolic, not mechanical

Monomyth stages should be treated as symbolic motifs, not required beats.

## Context first

Language, cultural tradition, ritual context, historical context, oral/performance context, and authority notes must be preserved.

## Counterexamples matter

Non-fitting elements are not noise. They are evidence.

## Formula drift is a governance risk

The model flags stage literalism, beat matching, context loss, overfitting, and universal claim strength.
