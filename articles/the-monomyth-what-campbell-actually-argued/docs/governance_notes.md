# Governance Notes

Review monomyth claims when:
- formula drift is high
- specificity preservation is low
- source tradition is unclear
- ritual context is missing
- oral/performance context is missing
- authority notes are missing
- counterexamples are weak
- method limits are weak
- public consequence is high

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- monomyth pattern
- specificity preservation
- formula drift
- interpretation readiness
- revision recommendation
