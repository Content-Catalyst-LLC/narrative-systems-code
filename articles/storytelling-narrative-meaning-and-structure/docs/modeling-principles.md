# Narrative Modeling Principles

## 1. Begin with interpretation
Narrative structure becomes meaningful only within cultural, historical, symbolic, and ethical context.

## 2. Preserve sequence
Stories unfold through order, delay, reversal, recognition, and consequence.

## 3. Separate structure from formula
Patterns can clarify stories, but mechanical templates can flatten them.

## 4. Treat motifs carefully
Motif frequency does not equal symbolic importance.

## 5. Track transformation
Characters, communities, and worlds change through events, relationships, and choices.

## 6. Include ethical caution
Narratives can humanize, distort, erase, manipulate, or justify.

## 7. Respect oral tradition
Oral storytelling is performance, transmission, memory, and variation, not merely written text.
