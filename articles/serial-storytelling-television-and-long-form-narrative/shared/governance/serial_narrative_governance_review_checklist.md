# Serial Narrative Governance Review Checklist

- Does each episode work locally and contribute globally?
- Does the season have coherent escalation and thematic movement?
- Are unresolved arcs tracked responsibly?
- Is the continuity burden accessible to audiences?
- Are delayed payoffs foreshadowed and emotionally earned?
- Are ensemble characters given agency, interiority, and consequence?
- Is trauma repeated carefully or exploited for drama?
- Does the finale honor character, theme, world, and audience memory?
- Are AI-generated outlines reviewed for generic plotting and fabricated continuity?
- Is a human review owner assigned?
