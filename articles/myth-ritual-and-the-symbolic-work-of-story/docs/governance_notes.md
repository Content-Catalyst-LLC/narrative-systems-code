# Governance Notes

Review myth and ritual items when:
- symbolic interpretation is speculative
- ritual context is weak
- source tradition is unclear
- language notes are missing
- access control is weak
- totalizing order is strong
- scapegoating risk is visible
- exclusion risk is visible
- appropriation risk is high
- public consequence is high

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- symbolic function
- ritual context
- ethical risk
- interpretation readiness
- uncertainty
- revision recommendation
