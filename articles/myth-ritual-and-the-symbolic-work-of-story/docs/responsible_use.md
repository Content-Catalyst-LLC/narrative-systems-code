# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for community protocol, religious understanding, cultural review, legal review, language expertise, source criticism, or direct consent.

Use them to:
- audit symbolic function
- preserve ritual context
- identify ethical risk
- mark interpretive uncertainty
- create Catalyst Canvas-ready governance queues

Do not use them to publish restricted ritual material, appropriate sacred symbols, flatten living traditions into private psychology, or automate interpretive authority.
