# Modeling Principles

## Classify provisionally

Myth, legend, folktale, and epic are diagnostic lenses, not rigid labels.

## Truth claims differ

Do not force sacred, historical, imaginative, and heroic forms into one fact/fiction binary.

## Memory functions differ

Myths often carry origin memory. Legends often carry place memory. Folktales often carry social pattern memory. Epics often carry heroic and collective identity memory.

## Hybrids are common

Stories may mix mythic, legendary, folktale-like, and epic features.

## Governance matters

Traditional narratives may carry sacred, restricted, communal, or culturally sensitive material.
