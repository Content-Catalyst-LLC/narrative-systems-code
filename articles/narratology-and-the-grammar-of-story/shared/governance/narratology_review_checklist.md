# Narratology Review Checklist

- Are story and discourse separated?
- Are author, narrator, implied author, and narratee distinguished?
- Are voice and focalization distinguished?
- Is time mapped with order, duration, and frequency?
- Is character agency visible?
- Is information control documented?
- Are reliability and frame control reviewed?
- Are voice imbalance, closure pressure, and power blindness reviewed?
- Are method limits stated?
