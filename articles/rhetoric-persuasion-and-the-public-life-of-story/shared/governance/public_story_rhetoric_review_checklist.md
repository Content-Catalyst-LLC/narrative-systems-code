# Public Story Rhetoric Review Checklist

- Is the public situation clear?
- Is the audience identified?
- Is ethos transparent?
- Is pathos proportional?
- Is logos supported?
- Is evidence distinguished from example?
- Is identification inclusive and accountable?
- Is there scapegoating risk?
- Is emotion being used coercively?
- Are omitted perspectives documented?
- Does the story preserve room for judgment?
