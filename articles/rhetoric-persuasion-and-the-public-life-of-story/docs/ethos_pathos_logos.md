# Ethos, Pathos, and Logos

Ethos:
- narrator credibility
- witness standing
- institutional authority
- expertise and trust

Pathos:
- emotional force
- moral stakes
- grief, fear, hope, anger, compassion
- proportionality and restraint

Logos:
- evidence
- causal sequence
- comparison
- reasoning
- claim support

Review whether appeals are balanced and whether emotion or authority is replacing evidence.
