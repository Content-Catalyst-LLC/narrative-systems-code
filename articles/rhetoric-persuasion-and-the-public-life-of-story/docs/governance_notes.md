# Governance Notes

Review public story items when:
- public story risk is high
- verification strength is low
- emotional coercion is high
- scapegoating risk is high
- identity manipulation is high
- closure pressure is high
- representation sensitivity is high
- the story addresses vulnerable groups, public memory, legal claims, institutional legitimacy, civic action, or platform controversy

Governance outputs should document:
- review owner
- evidence limits
- audience risk
- emotional pressure
- identification pattern
- omitted perspectives
- representation concerns
- revision recommendations
