# Responsible Use

These workflows are research and editorial scaffolds. They are not substitutes for legal review, ethical review, cultural expertise, public-interest journalism standards, political judgment, direct community consultation, or verification.

Use them to:
- audit rhetorical appeals
- identify persuasive force
- flag ethical risks
- evaluate public story governance needs
- create Canvas-ready review queues

Do not use them to automate judgments about truth, civic legitimacy, legal responsibility, identity, trauma, or group blame.
